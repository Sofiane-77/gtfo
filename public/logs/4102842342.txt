Statement from Dr Gloria Rutermayer, WHO Regional Director for Europe

28th October 2053, Copenhagen, Denmark

Due to the continued spread of the NAM-V virus, the World Health Organization is recommending all European nations formally close their borders to all international traffic and trade. Existing social restrictions and policies should be maintained and where possible, complete isolation of communities no larger than 100 individuals should be enforced. WHO efforts to find a both the vaccine and an effective cure are ongoing in partnership with all global health organizations.

Keep yourself and others safe.
Wash hands regularly
Wear a mask
Practice Social Distancing
Inform local authorities of any known cases of NAM-V

Your commitment to following these guidelines WILL SAVE LIVES.

The objective of this Disease Outbreak News is to raise awareness, inform readiness and response efforts, and provide technical guidance for immediate recommended actions.
